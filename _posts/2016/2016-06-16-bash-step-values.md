This is cool, I can use this syntax:

~~~
{&lt;start&gt;..&lt;end&gt;..&lt;increment&gt;}
~~~

To step through numbers! Like this:

~~~
$ echo {1..10..2}
1 3 5 7 9
~~~

It works in loops, or any old thing! Awesome!
